Our fee structure is simple: we only charge when you make a sale. Success
fees are calculated as a percentage of your selling price (including VAT)
and cover all platform support, customer service, and transaction costs. 
If a customer returns an item, we credit the fee back to you - so you're never
out of pocket for returns.
Understanding your
success fees
Selling made simple, success made possible.
This document breaks down the success fees for all product
types. Go to the next page to find the product categories that
are applicable to you.
